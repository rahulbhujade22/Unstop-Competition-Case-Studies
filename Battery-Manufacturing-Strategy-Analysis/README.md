# 🔋 Battery Manufacturing Strategy Analysis (Executive Edge)

## 🔍 Overview

This project analyzes the strategic decision of a European truck manufacturer entering the battery manufacturing industry.

## 🎯 Objective

To evaluate market opportunities, ESG considerations, and supply chain strategies for sustainable battery production.

## 📊 Key Areas Covered

### 🌍 Market & Geopolitical Analysis

* Dependency on lithium, cobalt, and rare earth metals
* Strategic global supply challenges 

### 🌱 ESG & Sustainability

* Focus on reducing greenhouse gas emissions
* Sustainable manufacturing practices

### ⚙️ Technology Analysis

* Battery types:

  * LFP
  * NMC 811 (recommended)
  * Solid-state batteries

### 🚚 Supply Chain Strategy

* Diversified sourcing approach
* Strategic partnerships for stability

### 📈 Market Entry Strategy

* Phased investment approach
* Pilot projects before scaling

## 💡 Key Recommendations

* Focus on NMC 811 battery technology
* Invest in R&D and innovation
* Develop recycling programs
* Build sustainable supply chains 

## 📁 Files Included

* Case study presentation (PDF)

## 📌 Conclusion

The project provides a comprehensive roadmap for entering the battery manufacturing industry while balancing sustainability and profitability.
