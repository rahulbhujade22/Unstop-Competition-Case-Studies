# ⚡ EV Market Entry Strategy (GOVECS India Expansion)

## 🔍 Overview

This project develops a market entry strategy for a European EV company entering the Indian electric two-wheeler market.

## 🎯 Objective

To identify growth opportunities, design a go-to-market strategy, and build financial projections.

## 📊 Key Areas Covered

### 📈 Market Opportunity

* Indian EV market expected to grow significantly (CAGR ~27.6%) 
* Strong demand driven by sustainability and government incentives

### 🚀 Product Strategy

* High-performance electric two-wheeler
* 150 km range, fast acceleration
* 85% CO₂ reduction compared to petrol vehicles 

### 📦 Distribution Strategy

* 50 dealerships in key cities
* 10 regional distribution hubs
* Online sales channels

### 📢 Marketing Strategy

* Digital campaigns
* Influencer collaborations
* Traditional advertising

### 💰 Financial Projections

* Revenue target: ~$25 million (Year 1)
* Profit margin: ~15% 

## 💡 Key Insights

* Strong growth potential in Indian EV market
* Need for localized strategy
* Importance of distribution + awareness

## 📁 Files Included

* Case competition presentation (PDF)

## 📌 Conclusion

This strategy positions GOVECS as a strong entrant in India's rapidly growing EV market.
